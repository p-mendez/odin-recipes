Boxed Buttermilk Pancakes

Description

Every Saturday morning we make these pancakes to our kids. We sit down and eat breakfast together while we watch Saturday Morning cartoons on MEtv.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Mix
  • 3/4 Cup Milk
  • 1 Tablespoon Oil
  • 1 Egg

Steps

  1. Heat skillet over medium-low heat or electric griddle to 375Â° F.
  2. Combine all ingredients and stir until large lumps disappear (do not beat or over mix). Let stand 3-4 minutes to thicken.
  3. Pour slightly less than 1/4 cup batter for each pancake onto lightly greased skillet.
  4. Turn when pancakes bubble and bottoms are golden brown.
